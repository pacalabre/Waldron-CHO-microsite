    Design Thinking

    Karen Kocher
    Chief Learning Officer, CIGNA

    Friday, March 8th
    7:30 am - 10:00 am

    What is design thinking? In a nutshell it is the belief that by starting with a goal, understanding the customer’s empathetic drivers and conducting rapid prototyping problems can be solved and solutions enabled that deliver more value and are sustainable. Individuals and organizations that utilize design thinking put their energy into learning about the future, the customer and test and learn. Cigna has transformed key HR processes and built capabilities utilizing a design thinking framework.
